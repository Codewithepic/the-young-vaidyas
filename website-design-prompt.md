# The Young Vaidyas - Website Design Prompt

## Project Overview
Create a modern, professional Ayurvedic wellness website for "The Young Vaidyas" - a student-led Ayurvedic startup founded by passionate BAMS scholars. The website should blend traditional Ayurvedic wisdom with contemporary web design, emphasizing authenticity, quality, and personalized wellness consultations.

## Brand Identity
- **Name:** The Young Vaidyas
- **Tagline:** हृदय तिष्ठति संहिता !! (The Samhita resides in the heart)
- **Core Values:** Authenticity, Tradition, Quality, Student-led innovation
- **Color Palette:** Earthy tones (greens, browns, golds) with clean whites and subtle accents
- **Typography:** Traditional yet modern - serif for headings, clean sans-serif for body text

## Target Audience
- Health-conscious individuals seeking natural wellness solutions
- People interested in traditional Ayurvedic practices
- Young professionals looking for preventive healthcare
- Individuals seeking personalized consultation services
- Those preferring chemical-free, handmade products

## Website Structure & Navigation
1. **Home** - Hero section with tagline, featured products, and call-to-action for consultation
2. **About Us** - Story of the founders, mission, and traditional approach
3. **Our Team** - Profiles of Harshit Jain and Muskan Agrawal with their expertise
4. **Wellness Consultation** - Online consultation booking system with pricing and process
5. **Products** - Showcase of 4 main products with detailed ingredients and benefits
6. **Quality & Ethics** - Manufacturing process, ingredient sourcing, and quality standards
7. **Journal** - Blog posts about Ayurveda, wellness tips, and educational content
8. **Experiences** - Customer testimonials, success stories, and reviews
9. **FAQs** - Common questions about products, consultations, and Ayurvedic practices
10. **Contact** - Contact form, business hours, and social media links

## Key Features

### Wellness Consultation System
- **Booking Interface:** Calendar-based appointment scheduling
- **Consultation Types:** 
  - Initial Assessment (60 minutes)
  - Follow-up Sessions (30 minutes)
  - Product Recommendations
  - Dietary Guidance
- **Pricing:** Transparent pricing structure with package options
- **Process Flow:** Step-by-step consultation journey from booking to recommendations
- **Integration:** Video consultation capability (Zoom/Telehealth)

### Product Showcase
- **Product Cards:** High-quality images with hover effects
- **Detailed Pages:** Individual product pages with ingredients, benefits, and usage
- **Shopping Cart:** Simple e-commerce functionality for product purchases
- **Inventory Management:** Real-time stock updates

### Content Management
- **Blog System:** Article publishing with categories (Ayurveda, Wellness, Products)
- **Testimonial System:** Customer review submission and display
- **FAQ Management:** Admin panel for updating frequently asked questions

## Design Requirements

### Visual Design
- **Layout:** Clean, minimal design with ample white space
- **Images:** High-quality product photography, founder portraits, and Ayurvedic imagery
- **Icons:** Custom Ayurvedic-inspired icons or traditional symbols
- **Animations:** Subtle scroll animations and hover effects
- **Mobile-First:** Fully responsive design optimized for all devices

### User Experience
- **Navigation:** Sticky header with clear menu items
- **Search:** Site-wide search functionality
- **Accessibility:** WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
- **Loading Speed:** Optimized images and code for fast loading
- **Call-to-Actions:** Strategic placement of consultation booking buttons

## Technical Specifications

### Frontend
- **Framework:** React.js or Next.js for modern, fast performance
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS or styled-components for consistent design
- **Animations:** Framer Motion for smooth interactions
- **Icons:** Custom SVG icons or React Icons

### Backend (if needed)
- **CMS:** Contentful or Strapi for content management
- **Database:** MongoDB or PostgreSQL for user data and bookings
- **Authentication:** Secure user authentication for consultation bookings
- **Payment:** Stripe integration for consultation payments

### E-commerce Integration
- **Platform:** Shopify or custom solution
- **Features:** Product catalog, shopping cart, checkout process
- **Shipping:** Integration with shipping providers for product delivery
